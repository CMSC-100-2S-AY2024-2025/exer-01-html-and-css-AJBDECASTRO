Turn-based game

A turn-based strategy game developed for CMSC 22, featuring an underwater world where players control fish characters in epic battles. With a vibrant aquatic theme, players select three fishes each from a randomized selection pool. These chosen fishes then engage in head-to-head combat, where strategy determines the outcome. The game continues until one player emerges victorious with a remaining fish or both teams are eliminated, ending in a draw.

Spa Employee and Appointment Management System

This system, developed for CMSC 21, streamlines the management of spa employees and appointments through efficient data handling using linked lists. Users can add, edit, and delete records for both employees and appointments, providing dynamic and flexible data manipulation. The system demonstrates the practical use of linked lists, showcasing their efficiency in real-world applications like managing growing datasets. Designed with functionality in mind, it offers a user-friendly approach to data organization in a spa setting.

This system, developed for CMSC 12, provides a comprehensive solution for managing library operations, including tracking books, borrowers, and log entries.
